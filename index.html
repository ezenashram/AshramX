/ about

a community of founders and domain experts ideating, invalidating, starting and scaling enterprises through a systematic process - this is venture 3.0.




Our process is based on practice + science. We are founders who have built and scaled massive companies and we have completed scientific research with Dr. Anders Ericsson on “The Science of Entrepreneurship” to map the optimal process for building billion dollar enterprises.

We bring in exceptional founders and collaborate to ideate and vet opportunities and markets. We then go on a truth seeking mission and talk to domain experts and potential customers to see if an opportunity exists. If it does, we fund the company and help launch and scale it.
